Tribute to Nikola Tesla

"The present is theirs; the future, for which I really worked, is mine." – Nikola Tesla

Biography

Nikola Tesla (1856–1943) was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist. He is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system. Tesla's work revolutionized the generation and transmission of electricity and helped set the stage for the electrical advancements of the 20th century.

Key Achievements

  • Developed the AC motor and transformer.
  • Invented the Tesla coil.
  • Patented wireless transmission technology.
  • Contributed to the development of radio waves.
  • Designed early versions of radar and x-rays.
